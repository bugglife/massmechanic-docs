How MassMechanic Works

Simple, fast, and free. Get matched with verified local mechanics in minutes.

For Car Owners

Step 1: Submit Your Request

Fill out a quick form (takes about 60 seconds) with:

  • Your contact information (name and phone)
  • Vehicle details (year, make, model)
  • What service you need (brakes, oil change, check engine light, etc.)
  • Description of the problem
  • Your location (ZIP code)
  • How soon you need service

No account creation or credit card required.

Step 2: Get Matched Instantly

We match you with 2-3 verified mechanics who:

  • Serve your area
  • Specialize in your type of repair
  • Are available for your timeline
  • Meet our quality and licensing standards

Most mechanics respond within 2 hours - many within minutes.

Step 3: Compare & Choose

You'll receive contact information for the matched mechanics. Then you:

  • Contact the shops directly
  • Get quotes and ask questions
  • Compare pricing and availability
  • Choose the best fit for you
  • Schedule your service

You work directly with the mechanic - MassMechanic doesn't act as a middleman. No platform fees or markups.

Example: Brake Repair Request

  1. Sarah submits a request: "My brakes are grinding and the car pulls to one side. 2018 Toyota Camry, need it fixed this week."
  2. Within 10 minutes, she receives contact info for 3 local brake specialists
  3. She calls each shop, gets quotes ranging from $350-$450
  4. She books with the shop offering same-day service at a fair price
  5. Total cost to Sarah for using MassMechanic: $0

What Happens After You Submit?

Once you submit your request:

  • Our system identifies mechanics who serve your area and handle your repair type
  • Verified mechanics receive your request details
  • Interested mechanics respond and you receive their contact information
  • You contact them directly to get quotes and schedule service

The entire process typically takes less than an hour from submission to having shop contact information.

Why It's Free

MassMechanic is free for car owners because mechanics value high-quality customer connections. There are no hidden fees - you simply pay the mechanic's normal rates for the service performed.

Quality Standards

All mechanics in the MassMechanic network:

  • Hold valid Massachusetts business licenses
  • Carry proper insurance
  • Meet our service quality standards
  • Have been verified by our team
