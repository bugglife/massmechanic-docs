Service Areas

Where MassMechanic connects car owners with verified mechanics in Massachusetts.

Current Coverage

MassMechanic currently serves the following Massachusetts regions:

Greater Brockton Area

Serving Brockton and surrounding communities including Bridgewater, Easton, Whitman, Abington, and East Bridgewater.

Fall River Area

Serving Fall River and nearby towns including Somerset, Swansea, Westport, and Freetown.

New Bedford Area

Serving New Bedford and surrounding communities including Dartmouth, Fairhaven, Acushnet, and Mattapoisett.

Not Sure If We Serve Your Area?

Submit a request anyway! We're constantly expanding our coverage and may already have verified mechanics near you. If we don't serve your area yet, we'll let you know and add your ZIP code to our expansion list.

Expanding Soon

We're actively growing our network across Massachusetts. Check back regularly or submit a request to see if we've added coverage in your area.

What We Offer in Each Area

In all service areas, you can find verified mechanics for:

  • Brake service and repair
  • Engine diagnostics
  • Oil changes and maintenance
  • Battery and electrical
  • AC and heating
  • Transmission service
  • Suspension and steering
  • State inspections
  • General repairs
