How It Works (For Car Owners)

MassMechanic makes finding a reliable mechanic simple and completely free. No fees, no account required, no middleman charges.

100% Free for Drivers

There are no fees to use MassMechanic. We connect you with verified mechanics in your area who provide direct quotes. You pay the mechanic directly - we never charge you anything.

The Process

  1. Tell us what you need: Submit a quick form (60 seconds) with your vehicle info, the problem, and your location
  2. Get matched instantly: We connect you with 2-3 verified mechanics in your area who handle your type of repair
  3. Choose your mechanic: Review responses, compare pricing and availability, then book directly with the shop you prefer
