Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about using MassMechanic to find mechanics in Massachusetts.

Getting Started

Is MassMechanic really free?

Yes, completely free. There are no fees, subscriptions, or hidden charges to use MassMechanic. You only pay the mechanic for the actual repair work.

Do I need to create an account?

No. Just submit your request form with your contact information and vehicle details. No account or login required.

How long does it take to get matched?

Most customers receive mechanic contact information within 1-2 hours. For urgent requests, responses often come within minutes.

Do you serve my area?

We currently serve the Greater Brockton, Fall River, and New Bedford areas in Massachusetts. Submit a request to check coverage - we're expanding regularly. See our service areas page for details.

How It Works

How many mechanics will contact me?

Typically 2-3 verified mechanics will respond to your request. You'll receive their contact information and can reach out to get quotes and ask questions.

Can I choose which mechanic I use?

Absolutely! You're free to contact all the matched mechanics, compare quotes and availability, and choose whoever you prefer. You're never obligated to use any specific shop.

What if I don't like any of the matches?

You're under no obligation to use any of the matched mechanics. If none work for you, simply don't book with them.

Do the mechanics know they're competing for my business?

Yes. Mechanics understand that you may be comparing multiple quotes, which encourages fair pricing and good service.

Quality & Trust

Are the mechanics verified?

Yes. All mechanics in our network are verified for proper licensing, insurance, and business credentials. We personally vet each shop before adding them to the network.

Can anyone join your mechanic network?

No. We operate a curated, invitation-based network. We personally select and vet shops based on licensing, reputation, and service standards.

What if I have a bad experience with a mechanic?

Contact us immediately at support@massmechanic.com. We take quality seriously and will investigate any issues. Persistent problems may result in removing a shop from our network.

Pricing & Payment

Do I pay MassMechanic anything?

No. MassMechanic is completely free for car owners. You pay the mechanic directly for the repair work at their normal rates.

Are prices higher because I used MassMechanic?

No. You pay the mechanic's standard pricing. There are no platform fees or markups added to your bill.

How do I pay the mechanic?

You pay the mechanic directly using their normal payment methods (cash, card, check, etc.). MassMechanic is not involved in the payment process.

Services & Repairs

What types of repairs do you cover?

Our network handles all types of auto repair: brakes, engine diagnostics, oil changes, battery replacement, AC repair, transmission, suspension, state inspections, and more.

Can I use MassMechanic for routine maintenance?

Yes! Oil changes, tire rotations, inspections, and other routine services are all covered.

What if my car isn't drivable?

Indicate that in your request form. We'll match you with mechanics who can handle your situation, including mobile mechanics or shops that coordinate towing.

Do you work with mobile mechanics?

Yes, our network includes mobile mechanics who can come to your location for certain repairs.

Privacy & Communication

Will I get spam calls or emails?

No. Your contact information is only shared with the 2-3 mechanics matched to your specific request. We don't sell or share your information with anyone else.

How will mechanics contact me?

Mechanics will call or text the phone number you provided in your request. You may also receive emails.
