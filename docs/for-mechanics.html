For Mechanics

Information about MassMechanic's verified mechanic network.

Our Mechanic Network

MassMechanic operates a carefully curated network of verified auto repair shops across Massachusetts. We personally vet each mechanic to ensure quality standards and proper licensing.

Invitation-Only Network

Our network is selective and invitation-based. We seek out established shops with strong reputations, proper licensing, and commitment to customer service excellence.

What We Look For

Shops in our network meet these standards:

  • Valid Massachusetts business license and proper insurance
  • Established track record in the community
  • Commitment to quality service and fair pricing
  • Professional communication with customers
  • Specialization in one or more service areas

Benefits of Our Network

Mechanics in the MassMechanic network receive qualified customer connections from car owners actively seeking their services. Our platform handles the initial customer intake and matching process.

Interested in Learning More?

If you operate a licensed auto repair shop in Massachusetts and are interested in joining our network, we'd like to hear from you.

Geographic Coverage
